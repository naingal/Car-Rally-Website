Rally Membership and Benefits!


Membeship is not required in order to participate in the rally. All we need is the right car, and spirit of sportsmenship. Members of the rally club will advance peek of the rally before available to the public. Registering for membership is free, and in order to become a member, you first have to prove you have the right qualities, and recommendation from one of the existing members to become part of us.


In this season, rally events will take place in France, Italy, Spain, Germany, and Portugal.


Each rally will be comprised of :

- Special Stage : a section of closed road at a stage rallying event used for timed speed tests. And it is coordinated such that each competing racer begins after a set interval, to reduce the chance of impedance by other competitors.The driver with the lowest overall time for all special stages in an event is the winner. Each special stage is usually between 10 kilometres (6.2 mi) and 30 kilometres (19 mi) in length. Some stages may be as long as 50 kilometres (31 miles) in length.

- Transport Stage : Unlike all others, they are not driven on closed roads or tracks, but instead in normal traffic, where drivers have to obey the speed limits and other laws of the road. They are not timed and their purpose is basically to take cars from the finish of one special stage to the start of another.

- Power Stage : special stage that usually runs as the final stage of a rally. Unlike normal special stages, which are timed to a tenth of a second, the timing of the Power Stage is to a thousandth of a second.
