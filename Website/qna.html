This Website is about vintage and classic car rally events and about our club's goals and visions. Vintage was found in March of 1984 by our former president, Mr. Joe Generic.

The rally event for the winter of 2022 will start on 3rd of November, 2022, in Paris, France.

There's no immediate way to become a permanent member of our club. However, in order to become one, you first have to register yourself. After the registeration, you could purchase a ticket to the rally event and accquaint yourself with the existing club members, and should one of them think you have enough passion or ability to contribute to the industry as a whole, you'll be invited to the club.

As of 23rd of October, 2022, it is estimated that there are around 35 participants from all over Europe.

France, Italy and Spain.
